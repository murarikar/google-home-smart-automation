Routine Name: I'm leaving

Routine Trigger (Starter):
- Manual activation from Google Home app OR voice command: “Hey Google, I’m leaving”

Actions Performed:
- Turn off: Mood Light
- Turn off: AC3
- Close: Blinds4
- Turn off: Washer5
- Turn off: Outlet1

Traits Used:
- OnOff
- OpenClose
- Modes
- EnergyControl (if applicable)

Description:
This routine simulates an "away from home" automation. When triggered, it shuts down energy-consuming devices and adjusts the home environment to save power — perfect for when the user leaves home.

Platform:
Simulated in Google Home Playground with virtual devices.
